The Team

A family that rides
together, bleeds together

Stabby Rats Racing started the way most bad ideas in our family start: someone said “we should sign up,” and nobody had a good enough reason to say no.

We’re not a sponsored team. We’re not chasing podiums (okay, sometimes we are). We’re a family that loads too many bikes onto one rack, drives to the trailhead, and figures it out from there. Some of us are fast. Some of us are enthusiastic. Everyone finishes covered in dirt.
